How to Add Images in Photoshop

From Local Files

Import images from your computer with just a few clicks. Go to File > Open or drag and drop the image into Photoshop.

From Cloud Storage

Access your images from cloud storage services like Adobe Creative Cloud, Google Drive, or Dropbox directly within Photoshop.

Using Adobe Stock

Browse and purchase high-quality stock images from Adobe Stock to use in your projects.

From Mobile Devices

Use the Adobe Photoshop mobile app to transfer photos from your smartphone or tablet to your desktop application seamlessly.
